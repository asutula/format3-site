Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Format3 ("we", "our", or "us") is developed and published by Foo/Skou and Aaron Sutula. This Privacy Policy explains how the Format3 mobile application ("the App") handles user information.

Information We Collect

Format3 does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

The App does not require users to create an account, provide personal information, or connect to any external services. The App also does not include analytics tools, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, or any backend servers that process user data.

All functionality of the App operates locally on the user's device.

Camera Access

Format3 may request permission to access your device's camera. Camera access is used only to enable features within the App.

Images or video captured through the camera are processed only on the device and are not collected, transmitted, or stored by the developer.

Children's Privacy

Format3 is suitable for users of all ages, including children under 13.

The App does not collect personal information from any users, regardless of age. Because no personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted, the App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Apple may collect anonymized diagnostic information such as crash reports through standard iOS system processes. This information is handled by Apple in accordance with Apple's own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

Because Format3 does not collect any user data, no information is shared with third parties.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are made, the updated policy will be reflected by the revised "Last updated" date above.
